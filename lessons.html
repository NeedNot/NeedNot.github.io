LESSONS


LIFT3 eFoil demo/lesson:

Early season discount: $100/hr

Come try out the new LIFT3 eFoil, the best for beginners to learn to hover over the water.
You will be given a safety crash course and then you can get out on the water.
Beginners with previous surfing or other water sport experience can usually get up in minutes and off the water within 30 minutes.
But don’t worry total beginner riders can get up within the first half of their session too.

Foil surfing lesson:

Early season discount: $100/hr

If you want to start foiling successfully then you need to try our setup.
We have the most stable wing connected to a short and controllable mast bolted on to a buoyant board- the perfect setup for a first-timer.
All our lessons are done on a private lake in Fresno.
The lake stays warmer than Millerton and the average swimming pool.
All ages can come to learn and kids will have an easier time getting up on the eFoil.
